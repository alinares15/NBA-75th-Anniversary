WNBA

Learn the history

The Women's National Basketball League was founded in April 24, 1996. They currently have twelve teams inluding the Los Angeles Sparks, Washington Mystics, Los Vegas Aces, Seattle Storm, Connecticut Sun, Chicago Sky, Minnesota Lyx, New York Liberty, Phoenix Mercury, Dallas Wings, Indiana Fever, Atlanta Dream, Houston Comics, Sacremento Monarchs, Charlotte Sting, Cleveland Rockers, Miami Sol, Orlando Miracle and Portland Fire.
